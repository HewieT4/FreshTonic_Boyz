About Freshtonic_Boyz

The journey of Freshtonic Boyz began in the midst of the 2020 global pandemic, a time of unprecedented change and adaptation. While the world paused, two friends, Mothibedi and Thapelo (aka Skwidiano), found solace and inspiration in the art of DJing. Mothibedi started sharing his mixes online via Instagram, quickly drawing attention with his unique sound. Thapelo, with his inherent musical talent and passion, soon joined him, adding another layer of creativity to their burgeoning project.

Through collaborative learning and a shared drive to perfect their craft, they honed their skills in both DJing and music production. This period of intense focus and exchange solidified their vision, leading to the formation of Freshtonic Boyz, an Amapiano DJ/producer duo with a distinct sound and energy.

In 2022, fate intervened at an event known as Vaal Fornication. It was there that they crossed paths with their MC, a natural performer who had been developing his skills in hyping crowds since his school days. The synergy was undeniable. Recognizing a shared passion and a complementary set of talents, they decided to join forces. On January 17, 2022, the MC officially became part of the Freshtonic Boyz collective, completing the trio and elevating their performances to a whole new level.

Today, Freshtonic Boyz delivers electrifying sets that ignite dance floors and showcase their unique blend of Amapiano rhythms and infectious energy.
